About

Lina is a fast action game where you control a snake lost in a sea of stars!

The further you go, the more points you earn. It's as simple as that. Avoid your enemies and the different obstacles that stand in your way!

You'll love playing Lina with its really simple and addictive gameplay!

Features:

  • Addictive gameplay
  • Never-ending scroller - go as far as you can!
  • Play whenever you want - for 30 seconds or for 2 hours
  • Beat your friends and get the highest score!
  • 3 types of obstacles and 3 types of bonuses
  • Over 30 achievements to collect
